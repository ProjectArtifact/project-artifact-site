Meet the Characters

The year is AD 2257, and the U.S. megacorporation Quanticorps seeks verifiable evidence to determine the facts about humanity’s history. Through an AI-powered initiative called Project Artifact, corporate scientist Dr. Gideon Gates believes he’s found the key to unlocking Earth’s past. But when he examines an ancient spear, the software provides a background that’s much different from the previously assumed narrative. Gates begins to question everything he knows…or thought he knew. In Project Artifact, join Gates as he begins a perilous quest for answers in the midst of injustice, top-level secrecy, and unforeseen enemies. Will the truth prevail in a world that’s determined to suppress it by any means necessary?
